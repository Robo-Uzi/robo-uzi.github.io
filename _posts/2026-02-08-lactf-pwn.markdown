## tic-tac-no

**Category**: pwn

**Author**: pstorm

**Description**: Tic-tac-toe is a draw when played perfectly. Can you be more perfect than my perfect bot? `nc chall.lac.tf 30001`

I get the challenge files. Contents of `chall.c`:

The computer uses a perfect minimax AI to always block or win, making it theoretically unbeatable. The vulnerability is that `player` and `computer` variables are stored before the `board` array in memory.
- `player` is at 0x4050
- `computer` is at 0x4051
- `board` is at 0x4068

So `board[-23]` would be the `computer` variable:
```shell
index = (x-1)*3 + (y-1) = -23
```
Use `row=1, column=-22`.

This writes `'X'` to the `computer` variable. Then:
- When the computer makes moves it places `'X'` (not `'O'`)
- Both me and computer place `'X'`
- Computers perfect play gets three `X`'s in a row

Overwrite `computer` and get the flag:
