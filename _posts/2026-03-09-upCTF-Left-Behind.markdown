**Title**: Left Behind

**Category**: OSINT

**Author**: BoneTeixeira

**Description**: I was passing through this street when I stopped to take a photo. It’s a place people walk through every day, often without noticing much beyond what’s right in front of them. Somewhere along this street, a name has been left behind - quietly, deliberately, and meant to last. That name is what you’re looking for.

Format - `upCTF{name_surname}` Example - `upCTF{Jane_Doe}`

I reverse image search it and find it is `Carnaby Street` in London. 

I found the name by going to this street on google maps and finding the `John Stephen Green Plaque` located at `1 Carnaby St`.
